# Welcome to Mesozoic Labs

We're excited to announce Mesozoic Labs—an open-source platform for building robotic dinosaurs through simulation and reinforcement learning.

<!-- truncate -->

## Our Mission

Bringing prehistoric creatures back to life as robots. We believe that by studying dinosaur locomotion, we can advance the field of legged robotics and create machines that move with natural grace and efficiency.

## What We're Building

- **Physics Simulations** - Accurate MuJoCo models of dinosaur anatomy
- **RL Training** - State-of-the-art algorithms for learning locomotion
- **Real Robots** - Transferring learned behaviors to physical hardware

## Stay Tuned

This is just the beginning. Follow our progress on [GitHub](https://github.com/kuds/mesozoic-labs) and star the repository to get updates.
